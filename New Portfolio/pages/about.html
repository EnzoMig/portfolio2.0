/hello_and_welcome

Glad you could make it! I’m Enzo, a senior in the Urban Technology degree program. My studies are a culmination of understanding cities, design, and code in order to effectively change how the cities of tomorrow operate. Through my curriculum, I have become proficient in design fundamentals, design processes, data analysis, user/interaction experience, UI design, and research. I am also studying Complex Systems where I model and analyze disparate systems and networks to observe how they function and interact with each other.

My academic focus has only increased my passion for technology. I am always curious about how the world works and if I can make it better, for both system efficiency and human interactivity. Being comfortable with my skills allows me to test my ideas real time and make iterations to improve the overall quality. My projects are been both a reflection of my course work and personal budding interest projects.

Please keep in through LinkedIn, email, or through the force. Until we meet again, thanks for stopping by! ✌︎︎
