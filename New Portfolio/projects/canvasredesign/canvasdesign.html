Summary

For this project, our cohort decided to look into the course management software used by our school known as Canva. This was a project designed to look at the different steps students would take to complete a given task within the application and resign the task flow to be more efficient. I first interviewed two students where they had to edit and click a link in my student biography. After that, I created mid-fidelity task flows to analyze the steps and create an improved task flow. I then conducted user testing on my new task flow and made the necessary changes. Below are some pictures of the end result.
